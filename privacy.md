# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 20, 2026**

TabTonic is developed by Steve Gillis. This Privacy Policy explains how the
app handles your information. In short: it doesn't collect any.

## Information We Collect

TabTonic does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The
app runs entirely on your device. Specifically, the app:

- Does not collect any personal information
- Does not require an account, login, or registration
- Does not access the internet or connect to any servers
- Does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies
- Does not include third-party SDKs that collect data

Any tablature, files, or content you create or import stays on your device and
is never transmitted anywhere.

## Third Parties

TabTonic does not share data with third parties, because it does not collect
any data to share.

## Children's Privacy

TabTonic does not collect personal information from anyone, including children
under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this policy will be updated and the
"Last updated" date above will be revised.
