## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does TabTonic require an internet connection?**
No. TabTonic runs entirely on your device and never connects to the internet.

**Where is my tablature stored?**
All tabs and files you create or import stay on your device. Nothing is
uploaded anywhere.

**How do I report a bug or request a feature?**
Email me at stevemg@gmail.com with a description of the issue or idea.
